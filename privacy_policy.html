Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2026-03-15  |  Developer: EXSports Oy  |  Contact: [email protected]

This Privacy Policy applies to the application developed by EXSports Oy (hereinafter "Service Provider") and describes how we collect, use, and protect your information. The application is provided as a commercial service.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

The application does not require account registration. However, if you contact us for support, we may retain your name and email address for the purpose of responding to your inquiry.

1.2 Information Collected Automatically

With your consent, the application may collect the following information automatically:

  • Device type and model
  • Mobile operating system and version
  • Anonymous usage statistics (features used, session length)
  • Crash reports (device state, stack trace at time of crash)

This data is collected only if you have consented during the app's first launch. You can change your consent at any time in the app's settings.

1.3 Subscription and Payment Information

The application offers a monthly subscription. All payments are processed exclusively by Google Play. The Service Provider does not collect, store, or have access to your payment card details or billing information. Subscription management (cancellation, renewal, payment method changes) is handled directly through the Google Play Store.

1.4 Local License Data

To enable offline functionality, the application stores encrypted license verification data locally on your device. This data contains only a timestamp of the last successful verification and an anonymized token hash. It does not contain personal information and is protected using Android's EncryptedSharedPreferences with hardware-backed encryption.

2. How We Use Your Information

Information collected is used solely for the following purposes:

  • To provide and maintain the application's functionality
  • To verify subscription status and enable offline access
  • To identify and fix technical errors (crash reports)
  • To understand how the application is used and improve it (analytics)
  • To respond to your support inquiries

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose beyond what is described above.

3. Location Information

The application does not collect precise or approximate location information from your device.

4. Artificial Intelligence

The application does not use Artificial Intelligence (AI) technologies to process your personal data or provide features.

5. Third-Party Services

The application uses the following third-party services. Each has its own privacy policy governing their data practices:

  • Google Play Services – app distribution and subscription billing
  • Google Firebase Analytics – anonymous usage statistics (consent required)
  • Firebase Crashlytics – crash reporting (consent required)

The Service Provider does not sell personal data to third parties. Data may be shared with the above service providers solely to operate and improve the application, and only to the extent necessary.

The Service Provider may disclose information if required by law, such as in response to a court order or government request, or to protect the rights and safety of users.

6. Data Retention

The Service Provider retains user-provided data for as long as you use the application and for a reasonable period thereafter. Automatically collected analytics and crash data is retained for up to 24 months, after which it may be stored in anonymized, aggregated form.

To request deletion of your data, contact us at [email protected]. We will respond within a reasonable time. Note that some data may be necessary for the application to function correctly.

7. Your Rights Under GDPR

If you are located in the European Union or European Economic Area, you have the following rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR):

  • Right of access – you can request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Right to rectification – you can request correction of inaccurate data
  • Right to erasure – you can request deletion of your personal data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Right to data portability – you can request your data in a machine-readable format
  • Right to object – you can object to data processing for analytics purposes at any time
  • Right to withdraw consent – you can withdraw consent for crash reporting and analytics at any time in the app settings

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at [email protected].

8. Consent

Upon first launch, the application will ask for your consent before enabling crash reporting and analytics. You may decline without affecting the core functionality of the application. You can change your preference at any time in the app's settings menu.

Continued use of the application after any updates to this Privacy Policy constitutes your acceptance of those changes.

9. Intended Audience

SurveyTools is intended exclusively for use by professional adults (18+). The application is not directed at minors. The Service Provider does not knowingly collect personal information from minors. If you believe a minor has accessed the application, please contact us at info@exsports.fi.

10. Security

The Service Provider implements physical, electronic, and procedural safeguards to protect your information. Locally stored license data is encrypted using Android Keystore-backed encryption. Access to any collected analytics or crash data is restricted to authorized personnel only. While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your information, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

11. Opt-Out

You can stop all data collection by uninstalling the application using your device's standard uninstall process or via the Google Play Store. You can also disable crash reporting and analytics at any time in the app's settings without uninstalling.
