Data Deletion Request

You can request deletion of any personal data associated with your use of the SurveyTools application. We will process your request within a reasonable time.

What we can delete upon request:
• Crash reports collected via Firebase Crashlytics
• Support correspondence tied to your email address

What is deleted automatically:
• Local license data — removed when you uninstall the app
• Payment data — managed by Google Play, not by EXSports Oy

What cannot be individually deleted:
• Firebase Analytics data — fully anonymized and aggregated, not linked to any individual user

To submit a deletion request, send us an email using the button below. Please include a brief description of what you would like deleted.

✉️   Send Deletion Request info@exsports.fi

We will respond to your request within 30 days. Note that some data (such as anonymized analytics) may not be individually identifiable and therefore cannot be selectively deleted.
