SurveyTools · Privacy

Data Deletion Request

SurveyTools stores your data on your own device, so most deletion is in your hands — but you can always ask us.

Delete data yourself

To remove all locally stored data, clear the app storage in your device settings or uninstall the app. This permanently removes inputs and preferences held on the device.

Ask us to delete

If you believe EXSports Oy holds personal data about you (for example from an email exchange), send a request to info@exsports.fi. Under the GDPR we will respond and, where applicable, erase the data within 30 days.

What to include

  • The email address or identifier you contacted us from.
  • A brief description of the data you want removed.

Request deletion

Email us and we will confirm within 30 days.
