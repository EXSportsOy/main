Heda · Privacy

Data Deletion Request

Most of Heda lives on your device, so deletion is largely in your hands — and you can always ask us to remove the rest.

1. Delete your diary (on device)

To remove all locally stored data, clear Heda's storage in your device settings or uninstall the app. This permanently removes your migraine entries and preferences held on the device.

2. Revoke Health Connect access

Open Google Health Connect on your device to review or revoke the data types Heda can read. Health Connect data is managed there, separately from the app.

3. Delete your account data

To erase the account data we process (your Google email, held via Supabase for authentication and premium verification), email info@exsports.fi from the address linked to your account. Under the GDPR we will erase it within 30 days, except where we must retain purchase records for legal/accounting reasons.

What to include

  • The Google email linked to your Heda account.
  • A brief description of what you want removed.

Request account deletion

Email us and we will confirm within 30 days.
