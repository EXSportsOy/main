Heda · Support

Report a Bug

Something not behaving as expected? A short, specific report helps us fix it fast.

What to include

  • What you were doing when it happened (for example logging an attack, signing in, syncing Health Connect).
  • What you expected versus what actually happened.
  • Your device and OS version, and the app version (shown in Settings).
  • Whether Health Connect was involved, and which data types.
  • A screenshot, if it helps — but please avoid sharing sensitive health details you would rather keep private.

Where to send it

Email info@exsports.fi with the subject "Heda bug report". We typically reply within a few working days. Anonymous crash reports may already have alerted us, but your context always helps.
