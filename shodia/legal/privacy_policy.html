Privacy Policy

EXSports OyLast updated 2026-05-26

Shodia keeps your training diary encrypted on your device. There are no accounts and no servers collecting your sessions.

Nothing leaves your phone unless you export it. Shodia has no cloud, no analytics and no tracking by design.

What we collect

EXSports Oy collects no personal data through Shodia. The app does not require an account, and your sessions, firearms, scores and notes are stored only on your device.

On-device, encrypted

Your diary is stored locally and protected by your device security, with an optional biometric or PIN lock. The data is not synchronised to any EXSports server.

Exports are yours

PDF and CSV exports are generated on demand and shared only through the destinations you choose (email, a file, an authority portal). EXSports Oy never receives a copy.

Store & payment data

Shodia is sold through Google Play. Google processes the purchase, trial and any diagnostics under its own privacy policy. EXSports Oy receives only aggregate, non-identifying sales information.

Your rights

Because we hold no personal data about you, there is normally nothing for us to export or erase. To remove all local data, clear the app storage or uninstall it.
