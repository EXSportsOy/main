Shodia · Guide

User Guide

Shodia is built to make a complete, trustworthy training record effortless to keep — and easy to hand over when you need to.

Logging a session

Add a new session and record the essentials: firearm, ammunition, distance, hit locations and scores. Sessions are timestamped and stored locally.

Tracking requirements

Set the requirement that applies to you — for example Finland 5 visits within 12 months for a firearms licence — and Shodia keeps a running count, so you always know where you stand. See the Disclaimer for the limits of this feature.

Exporting records

  • PDF — country-specific templates for authorities and instructors.
  • CSV — your full history for personal analysis, with no lock-in.

Exports are generated on demand and shared only where you send them.

Locking your diary

Enable the biometric or PIN lock so your diary opens only for you. Privacy is the default, not an afterthought.

Languages

Shodia is available in Finnish, Swedish, Norwegian, Danish, Estonian, Latvian, Lithuanian and English. It follows your device language where supported.
